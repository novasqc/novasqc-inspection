Questions Fréquemment Posées

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please contact us directly.

What is the procédure pour travailler avec NovaSQC?
There are 6 steps:

1. Le client envoie les détails de la commande and requirements to NovaSQC
2. Le client approuve the costs offered by NovaSQC
3. NovaSQC coordonne avec le fournisseur to make arrangements
4. Un inspecteur qualifié se rend sur place for on-site inspection
5. Illustrated report is issued with detailed inspection findings
6. Le client prend la décision on next steps: re travail, ré-inspection, expédition ou rejet
Y a-t-il d'autres frais derrière les prix indiqués?
No. All the prices you see on our website are all-inclusive prices. There are no hidden costs. It covers all related costs such as inspector time, traveling within the site area, meals, and the inspection report.
Mon fournisseur a recommandé sa propre société d'inspection. Should I use them?
Nous le déconseillons fortement. Les sociétés d'inspection doivent être tierces. Suppliers only recommend inspection companies they have relationships with — which means the inspector is likely to pass a bad order to maintain that relationship. NovaSQC works exclusively for you, the client.
Quelles certifications vos inspecteurs possèdent-ils?
Our inspectors hold internationally recognized qualifications. All inspectors have at least 4+ years of field experience. Certifications vary by region — contact us for details on specific inspector credentials in your area.
Dans quelles langues vos inspecteurs parlent-ils?
Our inspectors speak English, French, Portuguese, Arabic, Swahili, Hausa, Amharic, Shona, Zulu, and more. We match inspectors to your project location to ensure clear communication with all stakeholders.
Puis-je annuler ou reprogrammer an inspection?
Yes. You can cancel or reschedule your inspection up to 4pm local time the day before the scheduled date at no charge. For urgent reschedules, contact us directly and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
Et si je ne suis pas satisfait with the inspection?
Pas satisfait, remboursement intégral. If you're not satisfied with the inspection quality, we'll re-inspect at no additional cost or provide a full refund. Your satisfaction and the integrity of your project are our top priorities.
