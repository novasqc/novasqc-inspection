Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our inspection services.

What is the procedure for booking an inspection?
1. Fill out the booking form on our Contact page with your inspection requirements.
2. We will contact your supplier within 24 hours to confirm the inspection date and location.
3. Once the inspection fee is confirmed, we schedule the inspection.
4. You will receive the inspection report within 12 hours after completion.
Are there any additional fees beyond the listed prices?
No. All prices listed are all-inclusive. There are no hidden costs. The price covers inspector time, travel within the inspection site area, meals, and the inspection report.
My supplier recommended their own inspection company — should I use them?
We strongly recommend against this. A true third-party inspection company should never be recommended by the supplier — this creates a conflict of interest where the inspector may be pressured to pass substandard work. NovaSQC works exclusively for you, the client.
What certifications do your inspectors hold?
Our inspectors hold internationally recognized certifications including API 1169 (Pipeline Inspectors), CWI (Certified Welding Inspectors), NACE Coating Inspectors, and ISO 9001 Lead Auditors. All inspectors have a minimum of 4+ years field experience.
What languages do your inspectors speak?
Our inspectors speak English, French, Portuguese, Arabic, and Swahili. We match inspectors to your project location to ensure clear communication with all stakeholders.
Can I cancel or reschedule an inspection?
Yes. You can cancel or reschedule your inspection up to 4pm local time the day before the scheduled date at no charge. For urgent reschedules, contact us directly and we will do our best to accommodate.
