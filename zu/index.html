What is the procedure for working with NovaSQC?
There are 4 simple steps:

1. Fill in our booking form here on the website or email us directly.
2. As per the booking form received, we contact your contractor/supplier to confirm the inspection date and site address.
3. Once the inspection fee is confirmed, we perform the inspection as per the agreed schedule.
4. You will receive the inspection report within 12 hours after the inspection is finished.
Are there any other fees behind the listed prices?
No. All the prices you see on our website are all-inclusive prices. There are no hidden costs. It covers all related costs such as inspector time, traveling within the site area, meals, and the inspection report.
My contractor recommended their own inspection company. Should I use them?
We strongly recommend against this. Inspection companies should be 3rd parties. Contractors only recommend inspection companies they have relationships with — which means the inspector is likely to pass a bad order. NovaSQC works exclusively for you, the client.
What certifications do your inspectors hold?
Our inspectors hold internationally recognized certifications including: API 1169 (Pipeline Inspectors), CWI/AWS (Certified Welding Inspectors), NACE (Coating Inspectors), and ISO 9001 Lead Auditors. All inspectors have a minimum of 4+ years of field experience.
What languages do your inspectors speak?
Our inspectors speak English, French, Portuguese, Arabic, Swahili, Hausa, Amharic, Shona, Zulu, and more. We match inspectors to your project location to ensure clear communication with all stakeholders.
Can I cancel or reschedule an inspection?
Yes. You can cancel or reschedule your inspection up to 4pm local time the day before the scheduled date at no charge. For urgent reschedules, contact us directly and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
What if I'm not satisfied with the inspection?
Not happy, fully refund. If you're not satisfied with the inspection quality, we'll re-inspect at no additional cost or provide a full refund. Your satisfaction and the integrity of your project are our top priorities.
